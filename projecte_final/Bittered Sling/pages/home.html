Welcome!

Bittered Sling is a webpage completely designed and coded by Biel Altimira as a final project for the Web Development Course of jediUPC. The main idea of Bittered Sling is to serve as an API client from TheCocktailDB and be used as a browser for cocktails and drink recipies.

How can I use it?

Search Tab

Click on the search tab located on the navigation bar on top of the page to access the functionality.

Look it up

Use the searchbar to look up for your preferred cocktail or let us surprise you with a selection of drinks based on your filters.

Prepare it

Now just get the list of ingredients or the recipe to prepare the selected cocktail. Cheers!

How does it work?

The data used for this project is provided by TheCocktailDB which is a third-party, open, crowd-sourced database of drinks and cocktails from around the world. It provides a wide variety of information on every drink and easy access through a API hosted as a JSON server.

How can I find cocktails?

Search by...

• Cocktail name

• Category

• Ingredients

• Glass type

• Alcoholic

• Random drink

You can get...

• IBA category

• Ingredient list

• Preparation

• Proper serving

• Alternatives

As you can see, there are many different options to choose from. Therefore, unless you are looking for a specific group of drinks or a particular cocktail, we suggest you first have a look at our random selection of cocktails to get a grasp on how the tags and categories work. After that, you shall be able to move around by yourself!
